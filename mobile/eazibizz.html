EaziBizz

EaziBizz is an Itinerant-first eBusiness & Public Services Listing and Management System. It is being implemented in CRS under the auspice of the Cross River State Desination Management Organisation - the Cross River State Tourism Bureau.

EaziBizz Service; Is SMART path to business success - whether its goods or services. It is a sure path away from unemployment or underemployment to SELF employment. It is also the catalyst to creating mass employment.

Why EaziBizz?
  • A Sotware-as-a-Service and A Software-as-a-Business
  • Mobile first solution (services are fully accessible using the mobile phone among other personal electronic devices)
  • Cross-cutting technology (EaziBizz is accessible via Multiple Online and Offline channels - i.e. WhatsApp and Telegram messenger, Web, App, USSD and SmarText)
  • Offline functionality ensures owners of "dumb" phones can still access essential bits of EaziBizz Services.
EaziBizz System Features
  • Business Listing
  • Market and Business Intelligence service
  • Enterprise, interactive customer feedback service
  • Enterprise eInvoicing and ePayment (mobile wallet driven) system (advsncing financial inclusion) service
  • Integrated social insurance services
  • Corporate governance framework for consumers and traders powered by the EaziBizz IDentity management system.
  • Enterprise Customer Loyelty Reward service (all invoices are automatic raffle tickets)
Benefits
  • Offers FREE electronic listing of public services and businesses - itinerant, small, medium and large scale.
  • Exponentially improved access to business, with improved productivity and profitability.
  • Affords itinerant, small, medium and large scale businesses clear business and market INSIGHT.
  • Opens up businesses - itinerant, small, medium and large - to the global market.
  • Provides consumers smart access to market.
  • Advances a centralised, integrated Customer Loyalty Reward system.
  • Advances a cashless economy - for the banked and unbanked - using the enteprise, integrated EaziBizz eInvoicing and mobile-wallet driven ePayment system.
  • Entrenches market governance beneficial to all parties in the business supply and value chain.
  • Guaranteed finacial inclusion with all the benefits it attracts.
  • Advances social insurance schemes integration - i.e. Health, conbtributory pension and housing scheme
  • Creates digital ecosystem for SMART cooperative management.
